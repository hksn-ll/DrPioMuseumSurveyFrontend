Museum Exhibits & Features

Explore the rich history and artifacts at Museo Ni Dr. Pio Valenzuela

Old Clinic Diorama

A reconstructed scene of Dr. Valenzuela's medical practice with period instruments, showcasing his dual role as a physician and revolutionary.

Painting of Katipunan Discussion

An artwork depicting a significant meeting related to the Katipunan movement, capturing the intensity of revolutionary planning.

Dr. Valenzuela's Office

A preserved workspace showing the doctor's desk, tools, and personal items, offering insight into his daily life and work.

Ancestral House Model

A scale model of Dr. Pio Valenzuela's family home, displaying traditional Filipino architecture and family heritage.

Lecture & Reading Room

A multifunction area used for museum talks, exhibits, and research, serving as an educational hub for visitors.

Life Timeline of Dr. Valenzuela

A chronological wall display highlighting key events in his life and contributions to Philippine history and medicine.

Museum Lobby & Living Area

A welcoming space recreating the ambiance of a traditional Filipino home, blending historical authenticity with visitor comfort.

Garden Gazebo

An outdoor feature offering a serene spot within the museum grounds, perfect for reflection and appreciation of history.

Katipunan Printing Press Exhibit

A historic press used to reproduce Katipunan documents and publications, showcasing revolutionary communication methods.
